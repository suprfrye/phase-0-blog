What is pair programming?

  • A technique where two developers work together at one workstation.
  • One developer is the Driver the other is the Navigator.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

  • Allows for better design quality.
  • Increases knowledge and solidfies learning.
  • Prepares students for professional development environment.

Driver Navigator Comparison

  • The Driver writes the code.
  • The Navigator directs the Driver.
  • The Navigator also reviews the code as it's typed.
